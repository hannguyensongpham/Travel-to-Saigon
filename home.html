Places to Visit in Saigon, Vietnam

Saigon Post Office

Address: Cong Xa Paris, Ben Nghe, District 1

You can buy a lot of sounvenirs inside of the post office

Notre Dame Cathedral of Saigon

Address: Cong Xa Paris, Ben Nghe, District 1

The Cathedral was constructed by French colonisits from 1877 to 1880. It is in the heart of Saigon

Bui Vien Street

Address: Bui Vien street, Ward Pham Ngu Lao, District 1

This is a street famous for foreigners gathering to enjoy Saigon's night life

Ben Thanh Market

Address: Le Loi Street, Ward Ben Thanh, District 1

It is a symbol of Saigon. You can buy fresh food and it also has a lot of street food. At night, the flea market is super busy
